About The Sweet Spots

The Sweet Spots are a rising young R&B/Soul/Funk band based in San Luis Obispo, California. Inspired by modern artists like Bruno Mars as well as classic Motown records, they bring a fresh yet timeless energy to every performance. Known for their infectious grooves, rich three-part harmonies, and outstanding musicianship, The Sweet Spots have quickly become a local favorite along the Central Coast. From humble beginnings playing laid-back sets in coffee shops, they’ve grown to headline venues such as The Siren, Sensorio, and Libertine Brewing Co. While the band’s core features five members, they often expand with a three-piece horn section for their most high-energy shows. This versatility makes them equally equipped to perform at wineries, private events, weddings, bars, and large concert stages. Whether locking in tight harmonies or layering in bold horn lines, The Sweet Spots deliver an unforgettable live experience that keeps audiences coming back for more.
